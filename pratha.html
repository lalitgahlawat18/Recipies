Aalu pratha

Paratha is a popular, unleavened flatbread from the Indian subcontinent, typically made from whole wheat flour (atta) and cooked on a tava (griddle). It's known for its layered texture, which can be achieved through various folding techniques.

Ingredients

  • aalu
