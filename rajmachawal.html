Rajma Chawal

Rajma Chawal is a classic North Indian comfort food made with red kidney beans cooked in a spiced, thick tomato gravy, served with steamed rice. It is not just filling but also rich in protein, making it a wholesome meal.

The dish has a rich aroma due to the combination of onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. Often enjoyed on weekends and family gatherings, Rajma Chawal is loved for its warm, home-cooked taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rajma (red kidney beans)
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (cumin, coriander powder, garam masala, salt, chili powder)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  1. Soak rajma overnight in water, then pressure cook until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute, then add tomato puree and spices. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add boiled rajma along with some of the water it was cooked in. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Cook rice separately and serve hot with rajma. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
